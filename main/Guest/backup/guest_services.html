Our main activity

SNV Cleaning offer to provide commercial cleaning services such as Carpet & Upholstery Shampooing, Post-renovation / Initial Cleaning for new houses, External High Rise Cleaning for Buildings and Showrooms, Floors Cleaning & Polishing and General Cleaning for Moving in or Moving out around KL and Selangor. At SNV Cleaning, environmental friendliness or quality of cleaning is the beginning, not the end. We can ensure all of that with our team of experienced professionals that were specialised trained in these respective areas. All of our works have met statutory, environmental and self-regulatory requirements, which give worry-free confidence to our clients that we are a responsible cleaning service provider with perfectly eligible and up-to-standards operations. Staff and clients have the freedom to choose each other. They also have the freedom to stop working together. Our cleaners are professionally trained and highly competent in every aspect of professional cleaning services rendered. We aim to continuously improve ourselves and promise to deliver the best services to our customers.
