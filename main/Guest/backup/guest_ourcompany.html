OUR COMPANY

SNV Cleaning Services was established on 1st January 2017. SNV Cleaning Services provides both contractual and non-contractual cleaning services to office and house all around Kuala Lumpur. Today, SNV Cleaning Services has been established to provide commercial cleaning services such as Carpet Cleaning, Spring Cleaning before Festivals and General Cleaning for Moving in or Moving out. . We aim to continuously improve ourselves and promise to deliver the best services to our customers. Hopefully, we may be of service to your esteemed company next.
